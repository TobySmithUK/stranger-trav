WELCOME, TRAVELLER.

You’ve reached the edge of charted space! A place where memory fades, stars burn cold, and stories take on a life of their own.

This isn’t a blog. It’s not a portfolio. It’s just a quiet corner of the galaxy where a story lives: Stranger on a Strange Shore.

Born from a Traveller RPG campaign and written from notes, memories, and the madness of a group of friends who rolled dice every Tuesday night, it’s a full-length, character-driven tale of survival, mystery, and rediscovery.

The world was imagined. The choices were real. The dice were random and unforgiving!

What began as a game became something more, forged in fire, fate, and friendship, and brought to life with a little help from AI.

Think of it like a black box from a forgotten mission: cracked open, cleaned up, and retold one chapter at a time.

So, pull up a chair. Grab a good bottle. And enjoy!
It’s a mighty fine story.
